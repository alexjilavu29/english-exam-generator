New Feature: AI Question Reformatting

You can now reformat exam questions using artificial intelligence to generate alternative formulations while preserving the original meaning and gap words.

This feature can be found on each question's detail page by clicking the Reformat with AI button.

To get started:

  1. Configure your OpenAI API key in the AI Settings page
  2. View any question and click the "Reformat with AI" button
  3. Choose from three AI-generated reformulations
